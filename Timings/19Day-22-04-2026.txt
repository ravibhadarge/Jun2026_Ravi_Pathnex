LifeCycle Rules 
Life Cycle rules in terraform is used to control how a resource is created, updated and dstroyed allowing customisation of default behaviour during infrastructure changes.

LifeCycle informs the Terraform how to handle resources when change come in resource .

Default Behaviour -----> it deletes old resource first and then create new one ,
it creates Downtime.

Some LifeCycle rules 

1. create_before_delete
2. prevent_destroy
3. ignore_changes
4. replace_traggered_by
